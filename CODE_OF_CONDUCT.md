# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — contributors and maintainers — pledge to make participation in **NjordScan** a
welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that keep this an open, inclusive, and healthy
community — and to be especially kind and patient with newcomers and non-experts,
since this project exists for them.

## Our standards

Behavior that helps our community:

- Being respectful, kind, and patient.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Taking responsibility, apologizing for our mistakes, and learning from them.
- Focusing on what is best for the whole community.

Behavior that is **not** acceptable includes harassment of any kind, personal or
political attacks, demeaning or discriminatory comments, publishing other people's
private information without their explicit permission, and any other conduct that a
reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Our responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and will
take fair, appropriate corrective action — including editing or removing comments,
commits, code, issues, and other contributions, and temporarily or permanently
banning any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or
harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (the repository, issues, pull
requests, and discussions) and whenever someone is officially representing the
project in public.
